Set in 1988, during the height of Japan’s economic boom, the story takes place in the massive entertainment districts of Tokyo and Osaka. It follows two young protagonists — BrightSettings and ColorSettings — as they begin one of the most intense stories in series history...

This version also includes newly added scenes involving key characters that were previously unrecorded.

STORY

A Battle Begins to Decide the Next Ruler of Kamurocho

December 1988. In the entertainment district of Kamurocho, Tokyo, booming with the bubble economy, a large-scale redevelopment plan was underway. Numerous organizations were after the land rights, but one mysterious plot with an unknown owner was holding up the plan.

As the conflict over the "Empty Lot" intensifies, multiple factions collide. Eventually, the battle expands into a massive conflict involving even powerful organizations from the west. Three men find themselves swept up in the tide of fate...

As one era ends, a new one is about to begin — and their destinies are about to change forever.

FIGHTING STYLES SHOWCASES

Bright's Battle Styles
Rampage with overwhelming force and dominate the arena!
"Brawler" embodies overflowing fighting style, "Rush" features rapid consecutive punch-based attacks and graceful footwork, and "Beast" grabs objects and enemies in front of him and swings them around. Switch battle styles according to the situation and crush your enemies!

Color's Battle Styles
Through your ever-changing fighting style, send your enemies into the depths of fear!
The "Thug" is a brilliant and brutal character who doesn't hesitate to attack weak spots, the "Breaker" is a tricky character who incorporates breakdancing moves, and the "Slugger" is a merciless bat-wielding character. Make full use of these three battle styles and send your enemies into a sea of ​​blood!

Have fun in a city of desire, dazzling with neon lights and people!
