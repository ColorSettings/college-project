Intro

To those who don’t yet know the “Dragon”,
and to those who love the “Dragon”

“Yakuza 0: The Place of Oath” returns in a Director’s Cut version, now launching on Nintendo Switch™ 2!
Set in 1988, during the height of Japan’s economic boom, the story takes place in the massive entertainment districts of Tokyo and Osaka. It follows two young protagonists — Kazuma Kiryu and Goro Majima — as they begin one of the most intense stories in series history...

This version also includes newly added scenes involving key characters that were previously unrecorded.
And for the first time in the series, an online multiplayer mode called “Katsuage Online Battle” is here!

Story

A Battle Begins to Decide the Next Ruler of Kamurocho

December 1988. In the entertainment district of Kamurocho, Tokyo, booming with the bubble economy, a large-scale redevelopment plan was underway. Numerous organizations were after the land rights, but one mysterious plot with an unknown owner was holding up the plan.

As the conflict over the "Empty Lot" intensifies, multiple factions collide. Eventually, the battle expands into a massive conflict involving even powerful organizations from the west. Three men find themselves swept up in the tide of fate...

As one era ends, a new one is about to begin — and their destinies are about to change forever.
